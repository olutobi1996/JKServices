What We Cover

Professional Plumbing & Electrical Services

Safe, reliable plumbing and electrical systems are essential for every property. Whether you're dealing with a leaking pipe, faulty wiring, damaged sockets, or planning a complete installation, our experienced team delivers professional solutions built to last.

From small repairs to full property upgrades, JK Property Services provides dependable plumbing and electrical work for homeowners, landlords, and commercial clients throughout the region.

What's Included
  • Plumbing Repairs Leaking taps, burst pipes, faulty toilets, blocked waste pipes, and general plumbing repairs.
  • Bathroom & Kitchen Installations Sinks, taps, toilets, showers, appliances, and complete plumbing installations.
  • Electrical Fault Finding Quick diagnosis and repair of electrical faults, power issues, and circuit problems.
  • Lighting & Power Installations New sockets, lighting upgrades, outdoor power supplies, and electrical improvements.
  • Consumer Unit Upgrades Modern fuse board replacements and safety upgrades completed to current standards.
  • Landlord Safety & Maintenance Ongoing plumbing and electrical support for rental properties and property portfolios.
