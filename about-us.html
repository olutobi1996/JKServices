Who We Are

The JK Story

JK Property Services started over a decade ago with a single van, a toolbox, and a commitment to doing things properly. What began as a local handyman service grew — through word of mouth alone — into one of the most trusted property maintenance companies in the East Midlands.

We're not a national franchise or a faceless contractor. We're a family-run team who live and work in the same communities as our clients. Every job we take on is personal to us, because our reputation depends on it.

"We built this business on referrals. When someone recommends us to their neighbour, that means everything. It means we did something right."

— The JK Team

Today, we carry out hundreds of jobs every year — from small repairs to full property renovations — and we bring the same level of care and professionalism to every single one.

What Drives Us

The Values Behind
Everything We Do

01
Honesty Above All
We tell you what a job needs — not what sounds impressive. If a repair is straightforward, we say so. If something needs more work, we explain why clearly before we proceed.
02
Quality Without Compromise
We use quality materials and refuse to cut corners. Our 12-month workmanship guarantee backs that up — if something isn't right, we come back and fix it, no arguments.
03
Respect for Your Time
We show up when we say we will, every time. We understand that your time is valuable and we never leave you waiting with vague arrival windows or last-minute cancellations.
04
Transparent Pricing
The price we quote is the price you pay. No surprises, no add-ons, no ambiguous invoices. We believe trust is built on clarity — and that starts from the very first conversation.
05
Community First
We're local. We live here, work here, and care about the communities we serve. When you hire JK, you're supporting a local business that genuinely gives back to its area.
06
Treating Your Home with Care
We treat every property as if it were our own. That means protecting floors, tidying up completely, and leaving your home in better shape than we found it — every single time.
