What We Cover

Professional Property Refurbishment Services

Whether you're updating a family home, preparing a rental property, or adding value before selling, a quality refurbishment can completely transform a property's appearance, functionality, and market value.

At JK Property Services, we manage refurbishment projects of all sizes, delivering skilled workmanship, attention to detail, and a smooth process from planning through to completion.

What's Included
  • Kitchen Refurbishments Modern kitchen upgrades, installations, worktops, cabinetry, and finishing touches.
  • Bathroom Renovations Complete bathroom transformations including plumbing, tiling, fixtures, and decoration.
  • Plastering & Decorating Fresh, modern finishes that bring tired interiors back to life.
  • Flooring Installation Laminate, engineered wood, luxury vinyl, tiled flooring, and complete room upgrades.
  • Internal Alterations Room reconfigurations, stud walls, repairs, and improvements tailored to your needs.
  • Full Property Renovations Comprehensive refurbishment projects managed from initial planning through to completion.
